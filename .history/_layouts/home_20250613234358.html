Brief Bio

I am a postdoctoral research fellow at the Center for Cognitive and Brain Health at Northeastern University.

My work is funded by the NIH NIA K00 Career Award. Past support includes the ACM/Intel CDS Fellowship and a Google Scholarship.

In 2024 I earned a Ph.D. in Personal Health Informatics at Northeastern. Earlier degrees: Economics (Barcelona GSE) and Information Science (University of Michigan).

Interests

  • Delivering interpretable, actionable user-behaviour insight for industry partners using computational & behavioural science.

I welcome collaborations and enjoy mentoring students—feel free to reach out!
